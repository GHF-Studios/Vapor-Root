The Contract is an organizational umbrella for [[Contract Family]] entities across the [[Vapor Ecosystem]],
[[Spacetime Engine]], and the [[Loo Cast]] game.
It is not one monolithic mechanism.
Each contract family must have a corresponding runtime/backend that implements and supports that family's runtime
mechanisms.
Current primary families are the [[Capability Contract]], [[Modding Contract]], and [[USF Contract]], with
counterparts in [[Capability Runtime]], [[Modding Runtime]], and [[USF Runtime]].

Boundary caveat:
USF contract structure is internal to the Spacetime Engine stack.
It should not be interpreted as making USF a standalone Vapor-level product or directly replaceable ecosystem layer.
