Scale Support is the declared support relationship between a scale-aware semantic surface and one [[Scale]] coordinate.
For ordinary authoring, support should be declared positively: a capability or contract-backed surface states the scales
it supports.
Absence of a support declaration normally means unsupported for that scale, without requiring every unsupported
capability-scale pair to be listed explicitly.
Explicit denial may still exist later as a policy feature, but it is not the default declaration shape.
Scale-support declaration and validation rules are governed by the [[Scale Contract]] and consumed by USF-aware
capability/runtime machinery.
