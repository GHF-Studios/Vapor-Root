The Redistributable Mod Contract Source is the as-is packaged distribution form of the [[Mod Contract Source]].
It is a build-time development/dependency source package distributed through the custom registry workflow and may also
be included in Steam-distributed composites.
Package contents may include contract source plus supporting development materials such as docs, tests, or examples.
