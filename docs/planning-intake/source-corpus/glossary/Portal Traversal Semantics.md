Portal Traversal Semantics defines continuity rules when observers or entities traverse non-trivial spatial mappings
(for example portal links or wrapping worlds).
This concept is intended to preserve interaction and perception coherence across traversal boundaries and may require
coordination across [[Scale View]], [[Observer-Relative Simulation]], [[Entity Plane Split]], and [[Entity Proxy]]
behavior.
